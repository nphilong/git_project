FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest scoreline in the history of the FIFA World CUP qualifiers - and indeed in history of international footbal-was recorded on 11 April 2001, When Australia beat American Smoa 31-0. This legendary match also brought global renown for Archie Thompson , whose 13- goal haul set a new world record, which stands to this day, for an indvidual player in a single unternational match. And though the defeat earned American Samoa ignominy, so inspiring has their subsequent recovery been that it is now the subject of an acclaimed documentary, 'Next Goal Wins', showing across the world.
